Holistically Thinking  

We believe that reforestation is more than just planting trees. Therefore, we strive for an ecologically, socially and economically sustainable solution from which everyone benefits with our approach.

Effective Climate Action

CO2 Storage with 3-Fold Impact

Carbon is stored in every tree planted. The wood is later used in the construction sector and continues to bind some of the carbon, while new trees are already growing. In addition, the greenhouse gases that would have been used in construction with concrete and steel are saved.
